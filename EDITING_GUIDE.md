# How to Update Your Website

Everything here happens in your web browser. You cannot break the website from these screens — worst case, ask Dave.

## Logging in

1. Go to **tabithatalking.com/cms/**
2. Enter your username and password.
3. Forgot your password? Click "Forgotten password?" on that page and check your email.

Tip: while you're logged in, every page of your live website shows a small Wagtail bird icon in the corner. Click it, then "Edit," to jump straight to editing that page.

## Write a new blog post

1. In the left menu, click **Pages**, then click **Blog**.
2. Click **"Add child page"** (top of the screen).
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — the post's headline.
   - **Post date** — today, usually.
   - **Header image** — optional. Click "Choose an image," then upload from your computer.
   - **Tags** — optional keywords like `audiobook` or `behind-the-scenes`.
   - **Intro** — one or two sentences shown in the blog list.
   - **Body** — the post itself. Click the **+** button to add a section, then pick:
     - **Rich text** — normal writing (bold, links, lists).
     - **Image** — a picture inside the post.
     - **Video** — paste a YouTube or Vimeo link.
4. At the bottom, click the **small arrow next to "Save draft"** and choose **Publish**.

"Save draft" saves your work privately — nothing goes public until you hit **Publish**. Feel free to save drafts and come back later.

## Edit or fix an existing post

1. **Pages → Blog**, then click the post's title (or use the bird icon on the live page).
2. Make your changes.
3. **Publish** again (same arrow next to "Save draft"). Until you do, the live site keeps showing the old version.

## Take a post down

- **Hide it (recommended):** open the post, click the arrow next to "Save draft," choose **Unpublish**. The post disappears from the site but is saved — you can publish it again anytime.
- **Delete it forever:** in **Pages → Blog**, hover over the post, click the **⋯ (More)** button, choose **Delete**. This cannot be undone.

## Add a new demo reel

1. **Pages → Portfolio**, then **"Add child page."**
2. Fill in the title, pick a **Category** (Commercial, Narration, etc.), and upload your **audio file** (MP3 is best).
3. Optional: duration (like "1:30"), a short description, and a transcript.
4. **Publish.**

The demo listed **first** under Portfolio is the one featured on the homepage. To change which one that is: open **Pages → Portfolio**, click the **sort arrows** at the top of the list of demos, and drag your favorite to the top.

## Update the About page or headshots

- **About:** **Pages**, then click **About**. Edit your photo, bio, or credentials. **Publish.**
- **Headshots:** **Pages**, then click **Portfolio** (the page itself, not a demo). Scroll to **Headshots**, add or remove photos there. **Publish.**

## Pictures, in general

You can upload images right where you use them (any "Choose an image" button lets you upload). To browse everything you've ever uploaded, use **Images** in the left menu.

## Two things worth knowing

- After you publish, the **blog and portfolio pages update immediately**. The **homepage** can take up to 5 minutes to catch up — that's normal, don't re-publish.
- Blog comments are off on this site, and nobody can sign up for accounts. Fans reach you through the Contact page, which emails Dave's admin address.
